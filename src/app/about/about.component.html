Office Procedures

  • Only execs and those with explicit permission may enter the office.
    • This is because the office should currently only be used for storage and retrieval of APO's items.

  • Wipe down everything touched (including items you brought to store in the office) with the hand sanitizer and wipes near the door.
  • Sign out using this Google Form to potentially facilitate contact tracing.
